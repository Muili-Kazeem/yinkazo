TABLE OF KNOWLEDGE

In Yinkazo's role as the General Secretary in the outgoing administration, he's always made it his priority to ensure that all students are comfortable irrespective of their department.

Every department has had its fair share of inconsiderate policies made by the school management and the administration in which Yinkazo played a major role ensured that we liaise with the school management to ensure fair treatment for all the students.

One such incident was when the school management requested Physiology, Pharmacy, Pharmacology, Nursing, Radiography, Medical Laboratory Science, and Physiotherapy to make a new accommodation payment before the end of their second-semester examinations and even tied the payment to their eligibility for the second-semester examinations. Yinkazo played an active role alongside other members of the executive council, in mobilizing the presidents of the affected departments to appeal for an extension to the payment deadline. The management rejected the appeal and to ensure that students are not left stranded during examinations, Yinkazo took it upon himself to write a similar appeal to the management to stall the process of evicting the affected students, and he succeeded in stalling the eviction.

There was also the incident where the just-inducted MBDS students were locked outside their rooms, Yinkazo joined the president in making calls to the School Management to ensure that they were not denied entry into the hostel.

So, in Yinkazo's role in the outgoing administration, he has made it his utmost priority to be fair to students of all departments and will continue to do so.
